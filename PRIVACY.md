# Privacy Policy for Seeker Dash

**Last Updated: March 14, 2026**

Seeker Dash is built as an open-source tool for the Solana Seeker ecosystem. Your privacy is our priority.

### 1. Data Collection
We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), wallet addresses, or transaction history to any external servers.

### 2. Local Storage
All data processed by Seeker Dash is stored locally on your device. This data is used solely to provide the dashboard visualization and is never shared with third parties.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Seeker Dash connects directly to the Solana blockchain via Helius RPC/WSS nodes to fetch public on-chain data. We do not have access to your private keys or any Seed Vault-secured information.

### 4. Tracking & Analytics
We do not use any cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools.

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
